Chapter 3: Derivatives

Туынды: анықтама, ережелер, стандарт нәтижелер, жасырын туынды және жоғары ретті туындылар.

Key concepts

  • Definition: \(f'(x)=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}\)
  • Tangent line: \(y=f(a)+f'(a)(x-a)\)
  • Differentiability ⇒ continuity
  • Higher derivatives: \(f''(x), f^{(n)}(x)\)
  • Implicit differentiation: қарым-қатынасты туындылау

Rules

  • Power: \(\frac{d}{dx}x^n=nx^{n-1}\)
  • Sum/multiple: сызықтылық
  • Product: \((fg)'=f'g+fg'\)
  • Quotient: \(\left(\frac{f}{g}\right)'=\frac{f'g-fg'}{g^2}\)
  • Chain: \(\frac{d}{dx}f(g(x))=f'(g(x))g'(x)\)

Standard derivatives

  • \(\frac{d}{dx}e^x=e^x,\ \frac{d}{dx}\ln x=\frac{1}{x}\)
  • \((\sin x)'=\cos x,\ (\cos x)'=-\sin x,\ (\tan x)'=\sec^2x,\ (\cot x)'=-\csc^2x\)
  • \((\arcsin x)'=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}},\ (\arccos x)'=-\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}},\ (\arctan x)'=\frac{1}{1+x^2}\)

Examples

  • Chain: \(f(x)=\sin(x^2)\Rightarrow f'(x)=\cos(x^2)\cdot 2x\)
  • Quotient: \(f(x)=\frac{x^2+1}{x}\Rightarrow f'=\frac{x^2-1}{x^2}\)
  • Implicit: \(x^2+y^2=1\Rightarrow y'=-\frac{x}{y}\)
  • Log diff: \(y=x^x\Rightarrow y'=x^x(\ln x+1)\)

Problem set (10+)

  1. \(\frac{d}{dx}\sqrt{x^2+1}\). \(\frac{x}{\sqrt{x^2+1}}\)
  2. Tangent to \(y=\ln x\) at \(x=1\). \(y=x-1\)
  3. Implicit: \(x^3+y^3=6xy\). \(y'=\frac{2y-x^2}{y^2-2x}\)
  4. \(\frac{d}{dx}e^{\sin x}\). \(e^{\sin x}\cos x\)
  5. Higher derivatives: \(f(x)=x^3\). \(f'=3x^2,\ f''=6x,\ f'''=6\)
  6. \(\frac{d}{dx}\ln(\sin x)\). \(\cot x\)
  7. \(\frac{d}{dx}\arcsin x\). \(\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}\)
  8. Product: \(\frac{d}{dx}(x^2\sin x)\). \(2x\sin x+x^2\cos x\)
  9. Quotient: \(\frac{d}{dx}\left(\frac{\ln x}{x}\right)\). \(\frac{1-\ln x}{x^2}\)
  10. Chain: \(\frac{d}{dx}\cos(x^2)\). \(-2x\sin(x^2)\)
  11. Log diff: \(y=(\sin x)^x\) for \(x>0\). Use \(\ln y=x\ln(\sin x)\)
