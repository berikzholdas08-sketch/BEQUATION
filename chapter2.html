Chapter 2: Limits and continuity

Шектер, асимптоталар, үздіксіздік, теоремалар және классикалық шектер.

Key concepts

  • Limit: \(\lim_{x\to a} f(x)=L\).
  • One-sided limits: \(\lim_{x\to a^-} f(x)\), \(\lim_{x\to a^+} f(x)\).
  • Continuity at a: \(\lim_{x\to a} f(x)=f(a)\).
  • Infinite limits & asymptotes: вертикал/горизонтал асимптоталарды табу.
  • Limit laws, Squeeze theorem, IVT: шектерді есептеу мен бар болуын дәлелдеу.

Core limits

  • \(\lim_{x\to 0}\frac{\sin x}{x}=1\)
  • \(\lim_{x\to 0}\frac{1-\cos x}{x}=0\)
  • \(\lim_{x\to 0}\frac{e^x-1}{x}=1\)
  • \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\frac{2x^2+1}{x^2+3}=2\)
  • \(\lim_{x\to0^+}\ln x=-\infty\)

Examples

  • Removable discontinuity: \(\frac{x^2-1}{x-1}\to2\) at \(x\to1\).
  • One-sided mismatch: piecewise with left/right limits differing.
  • Horizontal asymptote: equal-degree rational ⇒ ratio of leading coefficients.

Problem set (10+)

  1. \(\lim_{x\to0}\frac{\sin(3x)}{x}\). 3
  2. Find asymptotes: \(f(x)=\frac{3x^2-1}{x^2-4}\). VA: \(x=\pm2\); HA: \(y=3\)
  3. Continuity: \(f(x)=\begin{cases}\ln x,&x>0\\ 0,&x\le0\end{cases}\). Not continuous at 0
  4. \(\lim_{x\to0}x\sin(1/x)\). 0
  5. \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\frac{\sqrt{x^2+1}-x}{x}\). Rationalize 0
  6. \(\lim_{x\to0}\frac{1-\cos(2x)}{x^2}\). 2
  7. Find \(L=\lim_{x\to\infty}\frac{5x^3+1}{2x^3-7}\). \(5/2\)
  8. IVT: show root of \(x^3-2\) in \([1,2]\). Yes, continuous and sign change
  9. \(\lim_{x\to0^+}\ln(x)\). \(-\infty\)
  10. Left/right limits: \(f(x)=\begin{cases}x+1,&x<0\\2x,&x\ge0\end{cases}\) at 0. Left 1, Right 0 ⇒ DNE
  11. HA of \(f(x)=\frac{x^2+1}{x^2-1}\). \(y=1\)
